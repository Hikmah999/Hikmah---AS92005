ABOUT

To have a sustainable environment we need Eco-friendly materials. This can mainly help the environment by using materials that can provide a sustainable outcome for building homes. By achieving this, we need Eco-friendly materials that can fulfill this goal. Now I will introduce you to 5 Eco-friendly materials that can be used for homes. The 5 Eco-friendly materials are bamboo, reclaimed wood, hemp, cork and straw bales. These 5 materials will help us build an Eco-friendly home and have an Eco-friendly future

Bamboo

Bamboo is a natural renewable material which makes bamboo a sustainable choice. Cutting down bamboo canes assumes the growth of new bamboo canes, which means that if you keep cutting and harvesting down bamboo canes then it will encourage faster growth. This will mean that we dont need to worry too much about bamboo when it comes to sustainability. This is also an eco-friendly material that does not harm the local ecosystem and wildlife. Therefore bamboo is a really sustainable and eco friendly material used to make ecofriendly homes in the future.
